Our Purpose

The SafaltaSetu Pathway

To ensure that every Army Brat has the guidance, resources, and network they need to succeed in the civilian world.

Personalized Mentorship

Connect with veterans and successful professionals who have walked the same path. Guidance that understands the unique military lifestyle.

Educational Support

Resources and scholarships specifically curated for children of defence personnel. We help bridge the gap between postings.

Community Network

Join a brotherhood and sisterhood of peers. A strong support system that stays with you, no matter where you are posted.
